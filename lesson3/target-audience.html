Description Of Target Audience

The target audience is anyone living in the Madison county area and is looking for anything to do.
This website will provide a place to look for jobs, news, wather, or a place to live in the Madison county area.

Persona 1

Taylor

Taylor is a first semester college student who is looking for a job and student approved housing.
She doesn't really know where to start so she goes on this website to look for that information.

Persona 2

Robert

Robert is looking for a job in Madison county and does not know where to start.
He finds this website that will hopefully point him in the right direction of where he could start looking for a job.
